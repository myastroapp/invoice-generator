# Free Invoice Generator

Create a clean, professional invoice and download it as a PDF in seconds — free, no sign-up, runs entirely in your browser.

**▶ Use it: https://myastroapp.github.io/invoice-generator/**

![Invoice Generator screenshot](screenshot.png)

## Features
- Live preview as you type: your business, the client, line items, tax, and notes
- One-click **Download / Print to PDF** (uses your browser's print-to-PDF)
- Automatic subtotal, tax, and total
- 100% private — nothing is uploaded; your invoice data never leaves your device
- One-time **Pro** adds your **logo**, sets your **brand color**, and removes the footer credit — so every invoice is fully your own brand

No accounts, no watermark on the numbers, no data collection.
