Privacy Policy — Invoice Generator

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This invoice generator runs entirely in your browser. Everything you type — your business details, client details, and line items — stays on your device and is used only to render and print your invoice.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your invoice data is never sent to or stored on any server.

Payments

If you unlock Pro, payment is handled by Stripe; we never see or store your card details. The unlock is stored locally in your browser.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted here.
