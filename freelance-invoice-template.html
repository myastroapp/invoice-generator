What a freelancer's invoice should include

  • Your name/business, email, and address
  • The client's name and details
  • A unique invoice number and the issue + due dates
  • Line items: a clear description, quantity/hours, and rate for each
  • Subtotal, any tax, and the total due
  • Payment terms and how to pay (e.g. Net 15, bank transfer)
